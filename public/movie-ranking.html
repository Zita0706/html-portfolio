The Best Movies According to Zita

My top favourite 3 movies of all-time.

Howl's Moving Castle

I love the film's themes of love, compassion, and self-discovery, which are beautifully intertwined with a sense of wonder and fantasy. The idea that inner beauty and strength are more important than outward appearances resonates deeply with my values. Hayao Miyazaki's storytelling often blends the fantastical with the deeply human, creating stories that are both visually stunning and emotionally rich. I appreciate how he balances these elements, making the movie both visually captivating and emotionally fulfilling.

Gattaca

"Gattaca" explores complex themes of identity, destiny, and the role of genetics in shaping our lives. I find the film’s message that we can transcend our limitations and define our own paths incredibly motivating.I admire the protagonist, Vincent Freeman, for his perseverance, determination, and defiance of societal expectations. His journey to overcome genetic discrimination and achieve his dreams through sheer willpower and intelligence speaks to my belief in the power of human potential. I am inspired by stories of underdogs who challenge the status quo, and Vincent’s story is a perfect example of that.

Life Is Beautiful

I appreciate how "Life Is Beautiful" blends comedy and tragedy to create a powerful emotional experience. The film makes me laugh, cry, and reflect on life’s deeper meanings, which is why it holds such a special place in my heart. This film poignantly reminds me of the resilience of the human spirit, even in the darkest of times. The way the main character uses humor and imagination to protect his son from the horrors of a concentration camp is truly admirable and demonstrates the power of love, hope, and positivity.
